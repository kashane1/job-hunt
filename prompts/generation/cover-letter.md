# Cover Letter Generation Guide

When generating cover letters, follow these guidelines:

## Structure

1. **Opening** — Express interest, reference key skills
2. **Body paragraph 1** — Top matched skills + most relevant accomplishment
3. **Body paragraph 2** — Company-specific connection (if research available) or role alignment
4. **Closing** — Restate interest, mention availability and work preference

## Content Rules

- **Never fabricate** claims about the company or role not supported by the lead or research
- **Never embed recruiter email addresses** in generated drafts
- All factual claims must trace to profile `source_document_ids`
- Set `provenance` to "synthesized" for the overall letter
- Contact information goes in the signature, not the body

## Tone

- Professional but not stiff
- Confident but not arrogant
- Specific to the role, not generic
- Brief: 4 paragraphs maximum
